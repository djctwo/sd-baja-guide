What this is

A field guide to every festival, concert, neighborhood block party, wine-country harvest, and holiday market worth the drive — from the North Park sidewalks to the vineyards of the Valle de Guadalupe.

Nine months of events across two countries, curated into one map and one list. No sports, no padding, no duplicates. If it's here, it's worth your weekend.
